Welcome to Nepa Futsal

Nepa Futsal is a futsal which is located in Chamati. Remain up-to-speed with the latest happenings in Nepal Futsal through our website (www.futsalsansar.com), Facebook (@Nepa Futsal) pages, as well as connectin g with the wider Nepal futsal community. In today's society with the emphasis on physical fitness, the role of sports has taken on increasing importance for both youth and adults. For many disadvantaged youth, sports are recreation and also the only viable alternative to the confines of their environment (gangs, crime, drugs, etc). Therefore it provides goodwill through physical fitness, and it promotes a productive outlet for the disadvantaged youth in our society. Let's keep together to promote this sports in our locality and whole nation with your valuable information, news, events with the common web platform.
